## Research Findings: Specific Questions

### Are any competitors doing NFT conversion of physical cards?

**Partially â€” but not like MohnSters.**

- **Arena Club** offers digital ownership tokens for physically vaulted cards, but these are NOT creative digital assets â€” they're just certificates of ownership
